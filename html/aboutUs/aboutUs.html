Who We Are

Our History

Founded in 2024 as Xoconostle.com, Xoconostle is changing the culinary world by providing the tools to share recipes and cooking tips, while celebrating the expertise of home cooks online. Since then, Xoconostle has become the world's largest community-driven food brand, providing trusted resources to more than 60 million home cooks each month. Every day, cooks from around the world publish recipes and inspire one another through recipe photos, ratings, reviews, and videos. The combination of the Xoconostle community with our team of editorial and kitchen professionals provides authority found nowhere else on the internet and has turned the brand into an indispensable resource for cooks of all skill levels.
